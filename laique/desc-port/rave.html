KNOW AMERICA


Description

The site will tell you about any business in an american city. You have to search like "burgers" in "newyork" or "real estate" in "los angeles". You can view the data with three filters if you want to see the most reviewed than click on that, then click on lets go. The resulted data will be filtered according to most reviewed.

CODE <>

React framework is uesd. The site is fetching data from Ravenous API. It is hosted in firebase.
