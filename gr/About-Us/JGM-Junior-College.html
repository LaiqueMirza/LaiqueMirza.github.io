JGM Junior College

JMF-Jana Gana Mana Vidyamandir School & Jr College

The Government of Maharashtra and Deputy Director of Education, Mumbai has given permission for our Jahnvis Multi Foundation’s JANA GANA MANA VIDYAMANDIR SCHOOL & JUNIOR COLLEGE OF ARTS, COMMERECE & SCIENCE to start Primary, Secondary and Higher Secondary classes vide Government of Maharashtra order No.SFS/P.N.97/SM-2 dated 23-08-2016 with all the faculties like Arts Faculty with English, Hindi, Marathi, German, French, Sanskrit, History, Geography, Political Science, Philosophy, Mathematics, Computer Science and Information Technology, Commerce Faculty with English, Hindi, Marathi, German, French, Secretarial Practice, Mathematics, Geography, Economics, Book Keeping & Accountancy, Organization of Commerce, Computer Science and Information Technology and Science faculty with English, Hindi, Marathi, French, German, Sanskrit, Geography, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Psychology, Sociology, Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology.

Jahnvis Multi Foundation, established the Vande Mataram College of Arts, Commerce & Science in August, 2009 with First Year B.Sc. Computer Science and First Year B.Sc. Information Technology and in July 2011 First Year Arts, Commerce and Science streams were added to it. All the Streams are affiliated to University of Mumbai. As per the Govt. of Maharashtra, order No.NGC 2016/(102/16)Mashi-4 dt. 04-07-2016 given permission to start B.A. (FILM, T.V. AND NEW MEDIA PRODUCITON) , B.M.M., B.M.S. B.COM. (ACCOUNTING & FINANCE), B.COM. (BANKING & INSURANCE) B.COM. (FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT), B.COM. (TRANSPORT MANAGEMENT) and M.COM. (ADVANCED ACCOUNTANCY).

The Library with 2209 books and journals made available for the students.The college is having Two Computer Centres cater to the needs of all the students. Well-equipped Chemistry, Physics, Zoology & Botany laboratories are available. The college NSS Unit is entrusted with social work and organizes activities throughout the year. SC/ST/DT-NT/OBC Freeship and Scholarship are available for the students. The college has a Gymkhana and a spacious play ground. Outdoor and indoor games are arranged for the benefit of students. We also have a well equipped gymnasium which serves as health centre for our students.

Womens' Development Cell caters to the emotional and professional needs of girl students. Grievance Redressal Cell for the staff and students looks into problems faced at the work place.
